MARK ZUCKERBERG

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg was born May 14,1984 in White Plains, New York, U.S.

He and his three sisters were born to Karen and Edward Zuckerberg.

His Father worked as a dentist and his Mom as a psychiatrist.

He attended Ardsley High School in Ardsley, New York and then he transfered to Philips Exeter Academy

He built a software program he called "ZuckNet" that allowed all the computers between the house and dental work to communicate with each other in the 1990's.

He later wrote a program he called CourseMatch. It allowed its users to make class selection decision which develops on the choice of other students, it also helped study groups.

In 2003, he later created a program he called Facemash. It allowed students select the best-looking person from photos of choice.

In January 2004, he began writing code for a new website. "Thefacebook" was launched February 4, 2004, located at Facebook.com

In August 2004, Zuckerberg, Andrew McCollum, Adam D' Angelo, and Sean Parker launched a competing peer-to-peer file sharing service called wireling which happened to be a Recusor to Facebook.

He launched Facebook in 2007.
